## About Us

Lioma is an innovative and powerful API application designed to enhance lung care and sustainable lung cancer detection practices through cutting-edge AI algorithms and ML models.

Lioma leverages a robust stack of modern technologies to deliver a seamless experience for its users and maintain high accuracy in disease prediction.

### Our Mission

At Lioma, we're on a journey to empower both plants and farmers. By harnessing the power of AI and combining it with traditional knowledge, we aim to provide personalized solutions for healthier lungs and joyful breathing experiences.

### Inspiration

*Lioma: Embracing Lung's Wisdom*

Lioma is inspired by the harmony and balance found in lungs. Just as lung transforms and heals, our application aims to transform lung care through technology.

Drawing on the wisdom of lungs, Lioma leverages advanced APIs to create an experience of profound joy in healthy breathing. By combining the power of technology and nature, Lioma ensures the flourishing health of your plants with just a click and upload of a photo of a sick human lung.

Our innovative approach bridges the gap between traditional wisdom and modern innovation, offering a harmonious blend that nurtures both the earth and the spirit.

### Why Lioma?

- *Accurate Disease Detection:* Lioma employs a powerful AI model trained on a vast dataset of images, ensuring reliable and accurate disease detection in human lungs.

- *Tailored Remedies:* Beyond detection, Lioma goes a step further by providing personalized remedies for identified diseases, addressing lung health issues effectively.

- *Holistic Approach:* By blending technology and the wisdom of lungs, Lioma provides a holistic approach to lung care, honoring both tradition and innovation.

- *User-Friendly Experience:* With an intuitive user interface, uploading an image and receiving insights from Lioma is a seamless and straightforward process, making plant care hassle-free.

- *Time and Cost Savings:* Lioma eliminates the need for manual research and guesswork in diagnosing lung diseases, saving you time, effort, and potentially costly mistakes.

- *Support for a Healthier Future:* By choosing Lioma, you're contributing to a greener, healthier planet where technology and tradition unite to create sustainable growth.

- *Feedback-Driven Improvement:* Lioma values user feedback, using it to improve its capabilities, features, and user experience, ensuring that it continues to meet your needs.
